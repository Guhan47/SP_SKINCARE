FAQ

What is natural hair oil used for?

+

Natural hair oil is used to nourish and condition the hair, improve its texture, and reduce dryness. It can also help in reducing hair breakage, promoting growth, and adding a natural shine.

How often should I use shikakai and hair oil?

+

Shikakai can be used as a shampoo alternative once or twice a week, depending on your hair type. Hair oil can be applied 1-2 times a week or as needed for added moisture and nourishment.

Can shikakai and hair oil be used together?

+

Yes, shikakai and hair oil can be used together for optimal hair care. You can apply hair oil to your scalp and hair before washing with shikakai, or use them separately based on your hair care routine.

What should I look for in natural hair oil?

+

Look for hair oils with high-quality, pure ingredients like coconut oil, argan oil, or almond oil. Avoid products with synthetic additives, preservatives, or fragrances.

How should I store my hair oil?

+

Store hair oil in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Make sure the bottle is tightly sealed to prevent contamination and preserve the oil's effectiveness.
